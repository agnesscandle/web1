THE INTRODUCTION OF NCT system

NCT DREAM


NCT DREAM is debuted on August 25, 2016 with the single "Chewing Gum", with seven members. NCT DREAM has been internationally recognised as among most notable teen-aged artists of their time, known for music reflecting the growth of the youth.

Although NCT DREAM was initially formed to follow as aged-based admisiion-graduation system in which members would move on from the unit after reaching the Korean age of 20, in 2020 SM Entertainment announced the admission-graduation system to be removed, and NCT DREAM will continue future activities as a flexible-style group with the seven original members.
