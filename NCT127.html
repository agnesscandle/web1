THE INTRODUCTION OF NCT system

NCT127


NCT 127 is the second unit. Their unit name is a combination of the acronym for Neo Culture Technology shich is the meaning for their parental group's unlimited members, and as the unit is localized in Seoul, hence the number "127" representing the longitude coordinate of the city. Originally debuted on July 1, 2016, the unit's original line up was composed of seven members. After several monthes, they added new members.
